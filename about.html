INTRODUCTION

I’m Denis Paul Mucioiu , a twenty-two-year-old web developer from Romania currently living in Timisoara.

I studied at the West University of Timisoara, where I learned to observe, collaborate, and empathize.
The digital world is my sandbox and I’m always hungry to create new user experiences. My long-term goal is to make technology easy to use and accessible to everyone. The motivation that drives me daily is to work with people and brands that inspire me.

WHAT’S MY MISSION?

To design functional digital experiences

My expertise covers the entire digital product design process for both websites and mobile apps, including brand and visual communication, user experience design and user interface design. I like building new products from scratch or optimizing existing ones so they perform better from both a user and business perspective.
